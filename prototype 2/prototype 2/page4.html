Left over meal is about stop wasting food. With this site you can input all your leftovers and make a new recipe with them!

Why we need to stop food waste?

It’s a huge problem, and it affects all of us.

Left over meals is our way of highlighting the importance of not wasting food. From the severe effects it has on the environment, to how it can deepen the hole in our pockets, food waste is everyone’s problem. So by using your leftovers and having little impact on the environment you can help the planet and make a cheap meal in one go!

It doesn't mind if you are a hard worker, don't have enough money for the meal or just love the planet. This is the solution to make a cheap and quick meal and help the planet!
